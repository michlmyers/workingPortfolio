About Me

I’m a motivated professional with 15 years of experience in the music industry looking for a position as a software engineer.

I’ve consistently excelled at cultivating relationships, managing projects, presenting and demoing applications to large groups, and working with data during my career. I enjoy solving problems and finding creative ways to accomplish goals. One reason I'm moving from music toward development is the types of problems I wished I'd had the technical expertise to tackle myself, the gaps I saw on the technical side of my job that I'm excited to work on filling. Most recently I’ve been cementing my programming skills by building browser based games with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery.

As a graduate of Berklee College of Music I was trained to exercise my creativity. In my Rutgers coding Bootcamp I’m learning the skills necessary to develop modern looking websites with flawless functionality. Web development is the perfect practice to combine my professional, educational, and creative experiences and further my career by creating user friendly, dynamic, and aesthetically pleasing applications and websites as a software engineer.
